# Bill of Materials

This is a simplified GitHub-friendly BOM summary derived from the final report.

## Major components

- 2x 65 mm load-bearing rubber wheels
- 2x 12 V geared DC motors (approximately 300 rpm)
- 1x L298N dual H-bridge motor driver
- 2x VL53L0X ToF sensors
- 1x ESP32-S3 development board
- 1x MG90S servo
- 1x TopHat board
- 1x Vive photodiode + conditioning circuit
- 1x 3S LiPo battery
- 1x USB power bank
- 1x 12 V to 5 V regulator
- laser-cut acrylic chassis parts
- caster, standoffs, and mounting hardware
